--- title: Angela Ariza description: Researcher Data Steward ---
Angela A has been working as a data steward at the eScience Group since 2022. She provides training and support for onboarding BAM departments to BAM's central research data management system based on the open source openBIS openBIS-software. Angela previously worked as a researcher at Charité as part of the Berlin University Alliance project to identify researchers' needs for managing research data in line with RDM best practices. The project aimed to develop collaborative RDM services for the four Berlin universities. Angela holds a PhD in Natural Sciences with a focus on stem cells and tissue engineering from the Technical University of Berlin. She conducted her postdoctoral research in the field of biomedical sciences with focus on cell and tissue imaging in the Department of Experimental Radiology at Charité.
